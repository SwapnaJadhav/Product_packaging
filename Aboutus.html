Overview

Mithila Packaging is a pioneer in Multilayer Co-extruded Packaging film in Southern India. Founded in 1987, since then the company has grown and to strengthened it's manufacturing capacities to provide packaging solutions to the ever growing vast field of flexible packaging.

The company obtained its listing status having its share listed in Bombay Stock exchange & Hyderabad Stock exchange in 1995. The company has gone for expansion time and again and presently has total capacity of 6100 Mts. P.a for blown film and 720Mts for laminates.

The Company believes in absolute transparency in its working and owing to it the Company has appointed three independent Directors with professional qualifications who guide the Company with ethics for sustainable development and growth, Mr. VSN Murthy, M. Com, ICWA, Dr. R. R. Pujari, PhD and Mr. Suresh Chandra Bapna, M.Pharma are on the Board of the Company in independent capacity. The Company has Mr. Pushp Raj Singhvi, L.L.B as the Director on the Board to guide the Company with all legal nitty- gritty coming across the way of the Company.

The Company has a highly professional Board and Management Executives working as a team with the aim to take the Company to the zenith.
